ABOUT ME

I am an experienced graphic designer, turned teacher, who is again turning her 25 years’ plus experience into a new career, as a UX designer and web site developer. My artistic abilities drive my love for design, while my curiosity is never satisfied, hence I am two thirds through a Psychology degree I started after qualifying as a teacher.

Having assisted children with special learning needs for the past few years, I am now looking at ways of making technology accessible to all, through smart design. I learnt coding during 2022 and plan to complete my full-stack developer training in 2023.

Who knows where this new journey will take me? Having reinvented myself before, I am excited to see what I am able to do with my newly acquired skills and the multitude of opportunities a career as a developer offers.
